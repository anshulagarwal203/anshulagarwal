Research

Grab Holdings (GRAB US) — Long Equity
Southeast Asia · Consumer Internet · June 2025

A long pitch on Grab, Southeast Asia's leading superapp. The market is pricing the business at 8–9x normalized 2029 EBITDA while treating Financial Services — a loan book growing 56% YoY with NPLs below 2% — as zero optionality. Paytm Payment Services — Research Note
India · Fintech · 2025

A deep dive into Paytm's payments segment covering merchant fees and take rates, device penetration across India's merchant base, consumer UPI market share dynamics, and the lending business. Conclusion: at 32x FY28 EBITDA the market is pricing in flawless execution in a business that is structurally losing consumer share and dependent on regulatory goodwill for its lending engine.
